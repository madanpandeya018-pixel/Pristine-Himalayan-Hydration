The Source

Untouched by civilization, our water is naturally filtered through layers of Himalayan minerals.

Everest Pure is not just water; it is a testament to nature's perfection. Every drop is collected using sustainable gravity-flow systems that preserve the natural molecular structure of the glacial ice. Our commitment to the mountain ensures that for every liter sold, we contribute to the Khumbu region's environmental preservation.


We utilize carbon-neutral transport to bring this "Liquid Diamond" from the roof of the world to your doorstep in Kathmandu or any capital city worldwide.
